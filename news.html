Ocean Day Ceremony

  • Running time: 120 min

Join us in celebrating World Ocean Day with a special beach cleanup and awareness campaign organized by Ocean Defenders. This event brings together volunteers, marine biologists, and community members to protect our oceans from pollution and overfishing. Activities include an educational session on marine life conservation, interactive games for children, and a massive beach cleanup drive along the coastline. Together, we aim to protect our blue planet for future generations.
